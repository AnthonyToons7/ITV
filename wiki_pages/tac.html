Tac

"Our world must be protected"

Appearance

Tac is a robot with a tv screen as a head. He wears a black hoodie, trousers and shoes. He can set his screen color to whatever color he wants, but he prefers white.

Biography

Tac is one of the warriors in the Cyber World. He was never respected by the rest, yet he was the strongest one among them...

Skills

Tac is able to corrupt the game and change your HP to a random number at the start of the game.
