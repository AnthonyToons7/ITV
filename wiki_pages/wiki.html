Void

"I will find a way..."

Appearance

Void is a human character with purple hair and eyes. He wears a dark purple shirt, grey trousers and black boots. He also wears a black hat, an eyepatch and a cape, which has his crest on it.

When losing his temper, his crest will appear on his hand, giving him more strength in battle.

Biography

Void was never the favoured child at home, always being outclassed by his sister. After being taken to a facility, he became a quiet, depressed boy.

Only after he lost his best friend, he became the fearless warrior that values nothing else but justice, seeking for revenge and finding a way to revive his best friend...

Magic

When Void uses too manic magic abilities in a battle, the magic will slowly take over his body, consuming his energy. He will gain blue marks on his face, the more magic he uses, the more marks he gets.
