Status effects

Bleed

Bleed is a status effect that takes effect every turn. Most of the time, it will stay for 2 turns. Lucky charm will negate this effect.

Paralyze

Paralyze takes effect as soon as it's your turn. It locks you out of your offensive attack options, only leaving you with the option to heal, buff, or use your lucky charm. This effect usually last a couple turns.

Stun

Stun is a straight forward status effect. It locks you out of doing anything, leaving you with the only option to skip your turn. Lasts for one turn. Lucky charm does not prevent this.

Poison

Poison is a weaker version of bleed, but lasts for a longer amount of time. Lucky charm does not prevent this.

Burn

Burn lasts for a very long time, dealing damage to both players. Lucky charm will not prevent this.

Pressure

Pressure is a status effect that can be seen in either a good way, or a bad way. Pressure increases the damage of your regular attack, but gives it a chance to miss. This effect cannot be negated by a lucky charm.

Anti-crit-shield

This prevents you from being crit for one turn.

Boost

Boost is a buff icon. When this appears, you or the opponent is buffed. Be careful when using it, because this effect can be negated with a lucky charm.

Explosive Overdrive

Explosive overdrive is an effect that activates when Void has used too much magic. The magic will consume him, taking controll of him and super boost him, but at the cost of his health, and not being able to use magic and special moves. You get 3 explosive attacks that are crazily boosted, gain a chance to crit, inflict poison, bleed and a chance to burn the opponent. Cannot be negated by a lucky charm.
