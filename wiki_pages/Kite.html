Kite

"I shall eliminate all of humanity"

Appearance

Kite is a small, angel with white hair and red eyes. She posseses one wing and a tiny halo above her head. She wears a white jacket, along with white trousers and white boots.

Most noticably, she has a black orb on her chest that is corrupting her. To cover the rest up, she wears bandages

Biography

Kite has sat in the same facility room as Void, being roomates with him. Though, when the outbreak occured, she was shot while trying to escape. She was sentenced to heaven, but since the facility was messing with her, something went wrong in the process of making her an angel and she became corrupted instead.

Magic

Kite is capable of doing all sorts of light attacks, trying to overwhelm her opponent with the amount.
