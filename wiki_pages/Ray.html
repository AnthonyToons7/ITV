Ray

"I'll catch you again... though, sorry for your friend"

Appearance

Ray is a human character with red eyes and white hair, hence the name Whitlock. He wears a dark grey shirt, covered with a grey jacket. He wears grey trousers and brown boots and a visor in front of his eye.

His X on his head is a mark that Void gave to him, indicating that Ray is a sinner, and that Void will come back to hunt him.

Biography

Ray works in a facility where mysterious anomalies are contained, keeping them secure and making sure an outbreak doesn't happen. He was pretty much a baby sitter for Void and Kite. When an outbreak started involving the 2, Ray had to shoot Kite to at least keep one at bay...

Skills

Ray is capable of using all kinds of guns, preferably using a pistol or a sniper rifle.
