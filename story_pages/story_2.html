After winning a tough battle, a mysterious portal appeared, with no one else left to destroy, Void jumped in it...



He wandered around, wondering where he ended up...



Blood was all over the grey, cold walls...



It looked... familiar, somehow



Void slowly started realising where he was...



He was back in the hellish facility, the same one from 4 years ago.



A guy with white hair and red eyes walked up to him



"Welcome back" he said, as he grabbed his pistol out of his holster...



"Didn't expect to see you here. Was living alone too hard for you?" he said.



Void clenched his fists. "I'm not here to deal with your business, but if you insist, I will"
